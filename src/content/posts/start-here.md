This is the first post on the new site. It can be replaced with a more personal introduction once the domain is connected and the publishing rhythm is clearer.

The goal is simple: keep a public notebook that is polished enough to share, but light enough that writing never becomes a production.

## What belongs here

This blog can hold short essays, project notes, travel observations, technical decisions, and links worth remembering.

The best posts will probably be the ones that explain a decision while it is still fresh. What changed? What was hard? What did the work teach?

## Publishing rhythm

Start small. One useful note every week is better than a perfect essay every season.
